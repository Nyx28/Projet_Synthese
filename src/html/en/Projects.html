Our Work Over The Years

What Have We Done

Over the years, we benefited over 15 000 families from the province of Ocoa. Since our foundation, we built 74 primary school which educate more than 7000 students.
In between 1962 and 2024

  • 12 421 Homes
  • 41 Irrigation Projects
  • 17 Water Reservoirs
  • 69 Greenhouses
  • Plantation of over 20 millions trees

Also, ADESJO supported by the bias of direct financial help or with their social programs, the construction of

  • 152 Rural Aqueducts
  • 13 Rural Clinics
  • Over 750 Latrines

Future Projects

Amongst the plan for the future we have, we have the increase of the quality of life in a specific poor village by the construction of 30 houses in the span of 3 years in the communities of La Mesa de Domingo and Los Negros
