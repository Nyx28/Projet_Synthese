Our Team

Structure of the organization

The structure of ADESJO consists of Assembly, a Management Board, an Executive Board, a Special Advisory Commision (CEA), Departments, the Administration and the Community Councils

Assembly

This is made up of volunteers from civil society, mainly communities. who identify closely with the rural ADESJO's work. They include representatives drawn from 84 Community Councils, different officials, and employees of ADESJO`s departments and divisions.

Management Board

This is the highest level of authority in the institution. It consists of eleven (11) members elected for two years by the Assembly. They meet monthly to draw up ADESJO's policies and to monitor the institution's activities.

Executive board

The Executive Director acts as the permanent link between the Management Board and ADESJO's different areas of work. He or she also represents the institution's president as a delegate at different events.

Special Advisory Commission (CEA)

This commission is made up of the heads of all ADESJO departments and some divisions. They meet weekly to plan activities and to report on different technical matters.

Community Councils

They are the basis and foundation of the institution. There are currently 84 Community Councils representing the same number of rural communities. Their delegates meet monthly to discuss the problems facing their communities and to develop solutions. As ADESJO's representatives in the rural areas of the province of San José de Ocoa, they act as the people' s voice and vote on their behalf through ADESJO's decision-making processes

Administration

This consists of the Human Resources Office, Financial Office and the Departments of Infrastructure, Natural Resources, Education, Health and General Services. They are directed by the Executive Director.

Human Resources Office

ADESJO contributes to improving the technical quality of work, as well as the employment and salary levels in the province there by helping raise the standard of living and quality of life of the families in Ocoa and contributing to the development of the Dominican Republic.

Financial Office

This office administers the financial resources together with the Executive Director. includes accounting, Internal and external Auditing Divisions.
