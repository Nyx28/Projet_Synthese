The Different Departments

Department of Infrastructure

ADESJO promotes the basic right of every family to live in a rural infrastructure with access to the basic essential services. To meet their essential needs, ADESJO is carrying out projects for the construction and repair of houses, rural schoolhouses, rural community centers, rural dlinics, latrines, aqueducts, and county roads. Solar panels and hydroelectric facilities provide clean energy to homes and rural clinics. Fog collection techniques are also used to provide clean water. Furthermore, rural TV and computer centers have been established.

Department of Natural Resources

ADESJO deals with serious environmental problems that area affecting our renewable natural resources, agricultural production and animal husbandry efforts by implementing modem and efficient production systems for the following purposes: the irrigation of lands by installing sprinkler, drip, and mini and macro-sprinkler systems with the agreement that beneficiary landowners must lend up to 50% of their land to landless farmers; areas are being reforested for protection and commercial purposes; coffee is being planted in upper , humid zones; fruit tree and wood-tree nurseries are being established to meet our needs for different plants. furthermore, different activities are being carried out for soil conservation purposes; vegetables are being produced in a tissue laboratory and in mini-greenhouses, primarily by women's groups: tanks are being constructed for fish farming efforts; different animals (cows, oxen, pigs, goats, hens and ducks) are being distributed; metal silos are being distributed to store grains and seeds; apiaries are being established by women's associations; and fruits trees planted along hillsides in combination with short-cycle vegetables and with irrigation systems.

Department of Education

ADESJO has organized 84 community councils; a Gender Division is promoting the redefinition of relations between men and women, empowering more women to become community leaders; community councils and women's associations are becoming incorporated; they are both involved in agricultural and animal production, as well as in mini-greenhouse, apiary and fruit tree projects. Since 1994, boys and girls in rural areas have been learning about the environment, natural resources, health, nutrition and human and christian education through educational summer camps held in the rural communities. Fathers and mothers, as well as facilitators and animators drawn from the same communities, Participate as support personnel for these camps. An adult literacy program is benefiting thousands of people in the rural areas. Furthermore, the department is providing other educational opportunities and informing local, national and international audiences about the activities carried out by ADESJO and its communities.

Department of Health

ADESJO is helping improve the health of the population by providing greater access to, and improving the quality of health services, in full recognition that health is an integral part of development. This department and the rural communities coordinates work with the Ministry of Health and Social Assistance for the execution of a primary and Preventive Health Care.Program, as well as the distribution of medication. The department also supports rural clinics and help equip and repair health care infrastructure in the province.

Department of General Services

ADESJO is enhancing access to the most remote communities in the province and surrounding areas by using heavy vehicles and other machinery. This department supports all areas of the institution and provides services to all the Community Councils in accordance with the plans that have been set.
